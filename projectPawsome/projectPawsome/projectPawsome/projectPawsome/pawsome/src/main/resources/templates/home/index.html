Problem Statement

The problem identified is the lack of accessible and convenient options for individuals who desire temporary dog companionship without the responsibilities of dog ownership. Current limitations, such as housing restrictions, travel schedules, or financial constraints, prevent pet lovers from experiencing the joy and benefits of spending time with dogs.

Proposed Solution

The proposed solution is to develop a Dog Rental Application that connects dog owners with individuals seeking temporary dog companionship. This application will serve as a platform where users can register, browse available dogs based on preferences and location, and book rentals for specific time periods.
